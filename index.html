Welcome to the Decentra World ($DWORLD)


Decentra World is a user-owned, Virtual world where you can play, explore, and interact with games and activities. You can also purchase parcels of land on which to build your own environments, marketplaces, and applications.

Mission

A passionate team of founders, software engineers, and professional traders, working towards creating a decentralized platform, that breaks down the barriers of traditional finance, making it more efficient, cheaper & inclusive for all in one platform. The execution of Decentra World's Mission begins with the Decentra World's Defi-App, which is going to enable experienced and inexperienced Defi users, to surf Defi in a secure and effortless environment, whilst providing an array of useful utility.

Play to earn concept

We are going to build Sci-fi and Ai technology game where you can play and earn $DWORLD token and also collecting rare NFT's. Decentra World game is a multiplayer Play-to-earn model with role playing game that rewards players with cryptocurrency tokens.
